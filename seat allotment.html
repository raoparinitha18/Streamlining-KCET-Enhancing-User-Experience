Seat Allotment

Candidates need to follow the steps mentioned below in order to access the KCET seat allotment procedure online:

  • Visit the official website at kea.kar.nic.in as per KCET seat allotment date.
  • Click on the link to allotment of KCET 2023.
  • Candidates will have to enter their Karnataka CET number.
  • KCET seat allotment 2023 shall be displayed on the screen.
  • Download the same and proceed with the further KCET seat allotment procedure.
